> Official executive summary and product vision for the Trading OS.

# Vision

Build a retail-first trading operating system with institutional-grade architecture that combines deterministic rule engines, machine learning, continuous learning, and future AI capabilities into a single, modular platform.

# Mission

Provide traders with a safe, transparent, continuously improving platform that emphasizes capital preservation, explainable decisions, and disciplined execution over speculation.

# Core Objectives

- Protect capital before maximizing profit.
- Standardize the complete trading lifecycle.
- Learn continuously through replay and paper trading.
- Support stocks, ETFs, futures, options, forex, and crypto.
- Remain extensible for future ML and LLM capabilities.
- Keep every decision auditable and reproducible.

# Guiding Principles

- Retail-first user experience
- Modular architecture
- Event-driven design
- Replay before Paper before Live
- Risk Engine is the highest authority
- Broker truth is authoritative
- Continuous improvement without compromising production stability

# Success Metrics

- Reliable execution
- Strong risk discipline
- Low operational latency
- High platform availability
- Continuous model improvement
- Complete auditability
- Positive user experience
