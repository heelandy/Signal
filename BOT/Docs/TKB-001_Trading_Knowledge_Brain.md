# Purpose

The Trading Knowledge Brain is the permanent memory of the Trading OS. It stores, organizes, connects, and retrieves everything the platform has learned from trading, research, machine learning, performance analysis, market behavior, and user interaction. It serves as the central intelligence repository that enables continuous improvement without forgetting prior knowledge.

# Responsibilities

- Store long-term trading knowledge
- Connect related information into a knowledge graph
- Preserve institutional memory
- Supply historical context to all modules
- Support replay and research
- Feed Machine Learning and AI Research
- Track knowledge evolution over time

# Core Philosophy

Nothing learned should be lost.

Every trade, experiment, model, decision, market condition, and lesson becomes part of the Trading Knowledge Brain.
