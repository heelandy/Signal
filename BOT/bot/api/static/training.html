📖 HOW EACH PANEL WORKS (click)
Run — buttons launch one research job at a time as a subprocess. Symbol select + timeframe select apply to dataset/ML/NN/heads. The log streams below; the status pill (top-right) shows what is running, its ET start time and elapsed. Results land in their own panel when the job finishes.
0·Data QA — validates the historical bar store (duplicates, bad candles, gaps, bars/day) for all symbols.
1·Rebuild dataset — replays history through the CURRENT rule version and builds the labeled candidate table (features + outcomes). 1b·Rejects — every blocked trigger with its reason + hypothetical outcome.
2·ML zoo / 3·NN zoo — trains 6 tabular / 6 sequence models with purged walk-forward; hard gates decide if anything may even become a PENDING champion.
4·A/B — old entry vs the standard, per symbol. 5·Sweep — 54 knob combinations ranked in-sample, judged out-of-sample. 6·Heads — tp2/stop/expected-R/no-trade models. 7·Similarity — pattern clusters. 8·Report matrix — slices + cost stress. 9·Parity — contract candidates ≡ engine trades.
Continuous training — background worker cycling dataset→ML→NN per symbol on your interval; ALWAYS --no-promote: gate-passers wait in "Pending models" for YOUR promote click. Shows current job + ET start time + per-job history.
Strategy approval ladder — the governance gate. Expand "WHAT you are approving" to see the exact rule version, what each stage means, and the evidence (backtest, parity, gauntlet, stress warnings). Paper autotrade stays blocked until the paper stage is approved; live also needs the lock file.
Pending models — gate-passing challengers awaiting your manual promotion to champion.
Data QA / L2-L3 panels — register a PATH (file or whole FOLDER; zip/zst read in place, auto-extracted in memory, never written to disk) or DRAG a file; Sync builds per-minute depth features that join onto candidates.
Dataset — headline of the labeled table for the selected symbol/timeframe. Latest training run — model-zoo charts vs the gates + buckets + calibration + full raw report expander.
Sweep — click any row for FULL details (what data/windows/rules were used) and to prefill the gauntlet. FULL GAUNTLET — the re-verify step: candidate vs adopted default, 7 checks; ADOPT only on 7/7.
Report matrix / Replay parity / Audit trail — evidence panels; audit is the append-only governance log.
Registry / Training runs — every model artifact + every run; click a run to load its full report.
