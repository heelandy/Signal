# Purpose

The News Intelligence Engine transforms raw news into structured, actionable intelligence that enhances trading decisions while preserving risk discipline.

# Core Philosophy

News is a market catalyst—not a trading signal by itself.

Every news event is evaluated alongside market structure, Security DNA, risk rules, and current market regime.

# Intelligence Pipeline

1. Ingest news
2. Normalize metadata
3. Classify event
4. Score expected impact
5. Link affected assets
6. Evaluate Security DNA sensitivity
7. Publish News Intelligence
