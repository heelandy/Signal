
# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

> Official Business Requirements Document for the Trading OS.

# Purpose

This document defines the business objectives, stakeholder needs, project scope, success criteria, and operational goals for the Trading OS. It bridges business requirements with the technical architecture.

# Business Goals

- Build a professional retail trading platform.
- Reduce trading errors through automation and risk controls.
- Improve trader consistency with analytics and learning.
- Support multiple brokers and asset classes.
- Enable future AI-driven enhancements without redesign.

# Project Scope

## In Scope

- Stocks
- ETFs

- Options
- Forex
- Crypto
- Replay Trading
- Paper Trading
- Live Trading
- Evaluation Accounts
- Funded Accounts
- Multi-Broker Support
- Machine Learning
- Continuous Learning
- Risk Management
- Portfolio Management

## Out of Scope (Current Version)

- Institutional OMS integration
- High-frequency trading (HFT)
- Autonomous live AI trading
- Cross-user social trading

# Success Criteria

- Stable production deployment
- Verified replay accuracy
- Successful paper validation
- Positive user adoption
- Reliable broker integrations
- High system availability

# Risks

- Broker API changes
- Market data outages
- Infrastructure failures
- ML model drift
- Regulatory changes

# Assumptions

- Users have supported brokerage accounts.
- Reliable internet connectivity.
- Historical market data availability.
- Modular architecture remains the implementation standard.
